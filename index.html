Mental health is a positive concept related to the social and emotional wellbeing of individuals and communities. Having good mental health, or being mentally healthy, is more than just the absence of illness, rather it’s a state of overall wellbeing. so just like a car needs fuel to keep going, your mind also needs some steady deposits like: Get enough sleep and rest as sleep affects our physical and mental health adversely. Take time for things that you enjoy and be active when it comes to physical fitness A healthy diet is must for your body balance and nutrition Nurture relationships and get involved Learn to manage stress and at the same time, don't take too much stress Set realistic goals and build your confidence And if you ever feel demotivated or demoralized, reach out for help. Everyone needs support from time to time, whether it be your friends, your family member, or even a doctor.

Studies state that ASHTANGA VINYASA YOGA is a healthy start to your day. When you do yoga, your brain cells develop new connections, and changes occur in brain struc­ture as well as function, resulting in improved cog­nitive skills, such as learning and memory. Yoga strengthens parts of the brain that play a key role in memory, attention, awareness, thought, and language. Think of it as weightlifting for the brain. Also, MUSIC THERAPY may help you psychologically, emotionally, physically, spiritually, cognitively and socially. People of all backgrounds, ages and cultures can respond to music, and to music therapy. If you’re dealing with a mental health disorder, music therapy can help you with communication and expression, help you explore your thoughts and feelings, improve your mood and concentration and develop coping skills.
